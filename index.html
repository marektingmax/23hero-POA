Strategy Overview

Three principles.
One plan.

23HERO is a no-BS men's grooming DTC brand in New Zealand. Four products, one bundle, and a category with almost no local DTC competition. Here's how we take it.

Budget Reallocation Rules
  • We may shift daily budget between campaigns by up to 20% autonomously — all changes documented in the weekly snapshot
  • Any campaign below 0.8% CTR (Meta) or 2% CTR (Google Search) for 7+ days: paused and budget reallocated — no exceptions
  • If Meta BOFU hits ROAS >3.0x by end of Month 2: reallocate $5/day from TOFU to BOFU
  • If Google PMax ROAS <1.5x by Day 45: reduce PMax by $5/day, shift to Category Search
  • YouTube and Competitor campaigns only activate when existing campaigns are spending in full
