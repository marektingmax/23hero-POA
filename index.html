01  /  Paid Social

Meta Ads Plan

Meta is the primary acquisition engine for the FEMIQN audience — women 45+ on Facebook and Instagram who respond to trust, storytelling, and real-women imagery.

Account & Pixel Setup

  • Confirm Meta Business Manager access and Pixel ID.
  • Set up standard events: ViewContent, AddToCart, InitiateCheckout, Purchase.
  • Set up a custom conversion for Purchase with revenue value passed through.
  • Enable Conversions API (CAPI) alongside Pixel for improved tracking accuracy — critical for this audience who may use older browsers or have tracking blockers.
CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE
Campaign 01

Cold Traffic · Prospecting (Acquisition)

Objective — Sales, optimised for Purchase.

Ad Set 1A Broad Australia · Women 40–65

No interest stacking. Let Meta's algorithm find buyers.

Budget — ~$15–20 / day

Ad Set 1B Interest-Layered Audience

  • Interests: women's fashion Australia, TVSN, plus-size fashion, comfortable clothing, body positivity.
  • Ages 45–65, Australia.
Budget — ~$15–20 / day

Ad Set 1C Competitor Audience

  • Interests: Birdsnest, Blue Bungalow, Sacha Drake, Cordelia St.
  • Ages 45–65, Australia.
Budget — ~$10–15 / day
Campaign 02

Retargeting · Warm Traffic

Objective — Sales.

Ad Set 2A Website Visitors (last 30 days, exclude purchasers)

Ad Set 2B Add to Cart / Initiate Checkout (last 14 days, exclude purchasers)

Ad Set 2C Instagram / Facebook Page Engagers (last 60 days)

Budgets — $5–10 / day per ad set depending on audience size. Creative should lean into trust, social proof, and the specific product viewed where possible.
Campaign 03

Retention · Past Purchasers

Objective — Sales.

Ad Set 3A Past Purchasers (lookalike seed list as it grows)

Ad Set 3B Lookalike 1% of Purchasers (Australia)

Note — Lookalike audiences will need time to build as the customer list grows. Start building the purchaser list from day one via CRM sync to Meta.
CREATIVE DIRECTION
Format Priority
  • Single image / carousel — hero products, benefit-led headlines.
  • Short video (15–30 sec) — real women, real fit, reassuring tone.
  • UGC-style content — if available, or styled to look authentic.
Messaging Framework
  • Lead — Trust + Fit. "A pant that fits — and stays comfortable all day."
  • Hook — TVSN social proof. "As seen on TVSN. Now available direct."
  • CTA — Shop Now · Find Your Fit.

Best-Seller "Our most-loved pant — back in stock."

Run 2–3 creative variations per ad set minimum. Test static vs. video, headline A vs. B.

02  /  Paid Search

Google Ads Plan

Google captures intent — the woman who has seen FEMIQN on TVSN or social and is now searching. Branded protection, non-brand intent, and Shopping are the three pillars.

Account Setup

  • Confirm Google Ads account access and link to GA4.
  • Enable auto-tagging.
  • Set up conversion actions — Purchase (primary), Add to Cart (secondary).
  • Import GA4 goals into Google Ads.
CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE
Campaign 01

Branded Search

Type — Search · Budget — $3–5 / day (protect the brand name).

Keywords

femiqn · femiqn.com · femiqn fashion · femiqn australia

Match Types & Bidding

  • Exact and Phrase match.
  • Maximise Clicks initially, then Target CPA once data builds.
Note — Even with a small budget this is important. Competitors may be bidding on the brand name, and the 45+ audience often searches for a brand they've seen on TVSN or social before buying.
Campaign 02

Non-Brand Search · Core Product Intent

Type — Search · Budget — $20–30 / day.

Priority Keyword Themes

  • women's pull-on pants australia
  • comfortable pants women over 40 / 50
  • women's elastic waist pants
  • flattering jumpsuits for women australia
  • women's clothing australia plus size
  • women's fashion 40s 50s australia
  • body positive women's clothing australia
  • ponte pants women australia

Match Types & Bidding

  • Phrase and Broad Match (monitored closely).
  • Maximise Conversions (once 30+ conversions tracked), else Maximise Clicks.

Negative Keywords (Day 1)

men's · kids · wholesale · cheap · fast fashion · teen · juniors

Campaign 03

Google Shopping

Type — Performance Max (PMax) with asset group focused on hero SKUs, OR Standard Shopping for more control · Budget — $20–25 / day.

Feed & Product Strategy

  • Confirm Shopify Google channel or GMC product feed is live and approved.
  • Hero products only — do not push the full catalogue to Shopping.
  • Product titles must be optimised: include fit descriptors, size range, key benefit.

PMax Asset Group Creative

  • Headlines — align to the messaging framework above.
  • Images — hero product shots + lifestyle imagery of women 45+.
  • Videos — if available, add 15–30 sec clips.
Note — If launching PMax, exclude branded search terms via campaign settings to avoid cannibalising Campaign 01.
Campaign 04

Remarketing · Display / YouTube

Type — Display Remarketing · Budget — $5–10 / day.

Audience

  • Website visitors (last 30 days).
  • Add to Cart (last 14 days).
  • Exclude purchasers.

Creative

Banner ads with hero product imagery + trust messaging. "Still thinking about it? Free shipping + 15% off your first order."

Future layer — YouTube can be added once creative video assets exist.
